All About Dogs

The dog is a pet animal.
A dog has sharp teeth so that it can eat flesh very easily,
it has four legs, two ears, two eyes, a tail, a mouth, and a nose.
It is a very clever animal and is very useful in catching thieves.
It runs very fast, barks loudly and attacks the strangers.

They are happy Animals

A happy dog's whole body can wag along with their tail.
A wriggling dog showing you their belly is likely to be a very happy and comfortable dog.
Each dog breed has a different relaxed tail position,
but a happy dog will generally raise it slightly.
